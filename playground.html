CRT Effects Active

This page demonstrates all active CRT monitor effects:

  • Scanlines: Horizontal lines with gentle drift
  • RGB Pixels: Subtle color separation
  • Vignette: Darker corners (look around the edges)
  • Curvature: Rounded screen edges
  • Monitor Shine: Subtle glare in top-right
  • Dust & Scratches: Minimal texture overlay
  • Phosphor Glow: Slight glow on headings
  • Subtle Flicker: Occasional screen flutter (12s cycle)
  • Glitch Flash: Rare scanline flash (15s)
